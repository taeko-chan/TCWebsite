A Minecraft Server Like No Other...

Taeko & Co is a unique server. It's like a seperate world with infrastructure and cities and countries.

It is the result of nearly two years of work (and counting!) by many people together. T&C is currently made

up of 3 countries, Atteca, Lyria & Swarovski, each with cities, countryside and extensive history.

As each country is run by seperate people, the focuses of each are different. I personally own Atteca, which

has towns & cities scattered around the country, connected by an extensive & realistic road network, with

the current large project being a new city called Wengen. Atteca's capital city, Atami, has been around

almost as long as the server has, and is ever growing.
